A small story behind the app

The idea came from a real personal need: my sister is an SMM specialist, and she needed an easy way to save and quickly find important links. But existing tools were inconvenient and confusing. Then my mom suggested a simple and effective solution – and I decided to bring it to life for @nicki_joga. Now you can also easily organize your links without unnecessary hassle.

Save and find links quickly. Especially useful for videos. Forget about saving them in chats or across different platforms. Now all videos from TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and other sources are stored in one place and conveniently organized for quick access.

I believe we are not the only ones who needed this app, so I’m happy to share it with you. I also have a few ideas on how to improve it, but maintaining and upgrading the app constantly requires resources.
